Hi! If you know why you are here, head on over to the /agora

This is a Discussion & voting system inspired by Liquid Democracy



The core Idea is that you can pick representatives - for single topics.
Your Representatives can pick people to represent them in the same or subtopics
Meaning your representatives have representatives, who have representatives, etc.
Particularly as you get deeper into sub-topics, a representative may be able to represent a massive number of people - through a chain of trust.
You are always able see what your representatives do, override their decisions - or replace them.
